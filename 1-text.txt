We must respect every person, no matter how pitiful and funny he may be. We must remember that the same spirit lives in every person as in us. Even when a person is disgusting* both in soul and body, one must think like this: “Yes, there must be such monsters in the world**, and one must tolerate them.” If we show such people our disgust, then, firstly, we are being unfair, and secondly, we are challenging such people to fight to the death.

    Whatever he is, he cannot remake himself. What should he do but fight us as enemies?
